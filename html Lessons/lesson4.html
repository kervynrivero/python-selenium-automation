Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1½ cups graham cracker crumbs, from 12 whole crackers
  • 5 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 32 oz (four 8-oz blocks) cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon packed lemon zest, from 1 lemon
  • 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice, from 1 lemon
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 6 large eggs
  • ½ cup sour cream

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Wrap a 9- or 10-inch springform pan with one large piece of heavy-duty aluminum foil.
  3. Press the crumbs into an even layer on the the bottom of the prepared pan.
  4. Bake the crust for 10 minutes, until set. Remove the pan from the oven and set aside.
  5. Reduce the oven temperature to 325°F. Set a kettle of water to boil.
  6. Make the batter: In the bowl of an electric mixer fitted with the paddle attachment or beaters.
  7. Beat the cream cheese, sugar, and flour together on medium speed until just smooth, about 1 minute.
  8. Scrape the bottom and sides of the bowl to be sure the mixture is evenly combined.
  9. Add the vanilla, lemon zest, lemon juice, and salt; beat on low speed until just combined.
  10. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing on low speed until incorporated, scraping the bowl as necessary.
  11. Mix in the sour cream. Make sure the batter is uniform but do not over-mix.
  12. Pour the batter on top of the crust.
  13. Bake until the cake is just set, 1 hour and 30 minutes to 1 hour and 45 minutes.
  14. Cool the cheesecake in the water bath until the water is just warm, about 45 minutes.
  15. Remove the springform pan from the water bath and discard the foil.
  16. If necessary, run a thin-bladed knife around the edge of the cake to make sure it's not sticking to the sides
